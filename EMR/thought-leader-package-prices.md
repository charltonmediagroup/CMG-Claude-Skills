# Thought Leader Package prices — Charlton B2B publications

This is the canonical source the `charlton-earned-media-report` skill reads in Step 4.5 to populate the Recommendations section. **Do not parse the per-publication Media Kit PDFs at runtime** — they are image-based and unreadable without OCR. This file is the authoritative price reference.

When pricing or entitlements change, update this file. The skill picks the right block based on `--site <ACRONYM>`.

## Package shape (identical across all five publications)

The Thought Leader Package is a three-tier programme. Entitlements scale linearly with tier:

| Entitlement | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Thought leadership article | 1× | 2× | 4× |
| Promotion in Partner Content | 3 months | 6 months | 12 months |
| Promotion in weekly newsletter | 3 months | 6 months | 12 months |
| Video interview | — | 1× | 2× |
| Social media posts (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, X) | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| LinkedIn boosting (targeted to publication's followers, 30 days) | USD 450 | USD 450 | USD 450 |
| LinkedIn audience credits (targeted to brand's audience, 30 days) | USD 500 | USD 500 | USD 500 |

LinkedIn-related amounts are quoted in USD across every kit regardless of the tier-price currency.
